About Us

DOM ENTERPRISE was established in the year 2017. We are the Supplier, Manufacturer and Trader of Grub Scrub,S.S & A105 MS & BRASS, Taper Plug, Metal Components, Brass Components, Steel Components, CNC & VMC Metal Components etc. Designed as per the customer's drawings & requirements, each of our products are made from stainless steel 304 / stainless steel 316 / EN-8. At our firm, we strive everyday to provide our customers quality products with world-class services.
